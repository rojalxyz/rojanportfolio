Project Overview

This project is an inventory management system designed to help admins efficiently manage products and track sales. Admins can add new products to the inventory, update product prices for future transactions, and view a list of products sorted by sales to identify the most popular items.

The system also stores order transactions, helping analyze business performance by showing whether it is operating at a profit or loss. Overall, the project aims to streamline inventory control and provide valuable insights into customer trends and product demand.
